About Fix My Campus

Fix My Campus (FMC) is the largest student-run advocacy organization at Duke representing over 2300 members of the Duke community. We are an affiliate body of Duke Student Government and are run by a committee of eight to twelve students who accept suggestions and requests from all students and seek to find ways to implement these changes. Our premise is simple: the quality of student life on Dukeâ€™s campus matters, and we must all work together to improve our community in any ways we can. As such, we work closely with Duke Student Government and the Duke administration on finding solutions to issues of all shapes and sizes.

FMC is organized primarily around our Facebook group. Students who choose to be members may post comments, requests, or suggestions on our page that our committee will then go through line by line and work diligently to achieve. In this process, we often work with group members themselves, and our best solutions often come from the constructive collaboration that occurs in our Facebook group. Each of us on the committee is here for a reason-- we are passionate about having a meaningful impact at Duke and trying to make the Duke student experience as close to perfect as it possibly can be - and we invite you to join us in this work each and every day.
